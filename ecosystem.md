## The Broader Work

Three public brands, one underlying architecture.

**Achieve Peak Performance** вҖ” human performance coaching. Athletes, adaptive athletes, combat sports, neurotrauma, return-to-sport.

**ValorGrid Solutions** вҖ” applied AI resilience research. The mechanism is Tacit-to-Technical Transduction: practitioner pattern recognition extracted into publishable frameworks through a structured human-AI process.

**Foundations & Fire** вҖ” the intersection of formation science and faith.

In the background: a private R&D hardening layer that protects, tests, and extends the public architecture. Not public-facing.

## DOI Gate

A public folder in this repo earns its place only when:

1. Permanent Zenodo DOI is issued
2. Paper PDF + `CITATION.cff` + `README.md` exist in the folder
3. `PUBLICATIONS.md` is updated

No empty folders. No pre-announced content.
